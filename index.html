What is DECOEN Tech

DECOEN Tech is one of India’s leading valves trading companies, specializing in the supply and distribution of high-quality industrial and instrumentation valves. As trusted agents and stockists, we offer a comprehensive range of valves including Ball, Butterfly, Gate, Globe, Check, Plug, Needle, DBB, Manifold, and Strainers—serving key sectors such as Oil & Gas, Refinery, Petrochemicals, Fertilizers, Water Treatment, Power, Process Industries, and Shipbuilding.

We represent top-tier global valve brands and maintain a robust inventory of mechanical valves ranging from ½” to 24” with pressure ratings up to 2500#, and instrumentation valves up to 10,000 Psi. Our stock includes valves in Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Low Temperature Carbon Steel, and exotic materials like Duplex, Super Duplex, Nickel Alloys, and Aluminium Bronze.

DECOEN Tech also supplies electrically and pneumatically actuated valves equipped with premium actuators such as Rotork, AUMA, and others. Our in-house testing capabilities and technical expertise ensure that every valve meets stringent project specifications. All products are delivered with certified documentation and backed by reliable after-sales support.

With strategically located warehouses across India and a growing inventory valued in crores, we are equipped to meet urgent project demands with speed and precision. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization, our dedicated QA/QC department maintains meticulous records and welcomes third-party inspections.

We are proud to serve a wide customer base across India and neighbouring regions, including major EPC contractors and industrial clients. Our commitment is to deliver quality products with minimal downtime and maximum reliability.
